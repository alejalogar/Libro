Who we are?

We are a high school located in Elche, Carrus.

Located in Spain in the Valencian Community, in the province of Alicante, for several years we have offered free and public education for everyone, we have a lot of professional modules with excellent teachers prepared for it.

The facilities are the basic for adequate teaching for all subjects, whether basic or extracurricular, let's talk more in depth ...

In our High School there are multiple study options such as: ESO, Baccalaureate, multiple FPs and multiple specialisation courses. ESO: Compulsory secondary education is part of basic education. According to the Spanish constitution (art.27.4) it is compulsory and free of charge. This stage has a duration of four academic years. Compulsory Secondary Education is organised in differentiated subjects, taking into account the needs and aims of the students.


FP: One of the best known is the professional family of IT and Communications which refers to the activities and companies whose objective is to implement, configure and manage computer equipment, services and components; the software with which they operate; the information repositories and the communication lines between fixed and mobile devices.


Baccalaureate: The Baccalaureate is the last stage of Secondary Education, it is voluntary and lasts for two years, normally between the ages of 16 and 18. It is carried out in different modalities, in a flexible way and it is possible to follow different routes within each modality in order to offer a more specialised training, taking into account the professional interests of the students.


Multiple specialisation courses: One of the specialisation courses trains you to define and implement security strategies in information systems by carrying out cybersecurity diagnoses, identifying vulnerabilities and implementing the necessary measures to mitigate them by applying current regulations and sector standards. You will become part of the IT security professionals who guarantee the protection of companies' most important asset: information. Get a versatile and up-to-date IT profile, train yourself.
